So why a bamboo bike?

Good vibes only

 

Stiffer than many carbon fiber frames

 

Several aspects of bamboo are extremely valuable to both cyclists and bicycle manufacturers: high strength-to-weight ratio, vibration control, and sustainable growth. Because of bamboo's tendency to grow straight, it does not exhibit "knots" and "turns" in its wood, unlike other types of wood. As a result, bamboo has a higher specific tensile strength than steel, as well as a higher specific compressive strength than concrete. This tendency also allows for excellent vibration control, which, in turn, provides for a smoother ride and increased stability on rough terrain.

Low carbon footprint

One of the biggest problems today is CO2 emissions that lead to global warming. This phenomenon affects every aspect of every living being on the planet. At DIVERA we combine our passion for art and bicycles while reducing the carbon emissions by making our bikes from ECO friendly materials.
With more than 1500 species, bamboo holds the Guinness World Record for the world’s fastest growing plant. In this respect, bamboo wins hands-down: it grows faster than many trees, growing up to 1.2 meters per day. By using bamboo as a main material for our bikes we not only replace other materials that require high amount of CO2 to produce, but we also prevent deforestation since bamboo grows fast and it rarely needs to be replanted. One hectare (an area roughly the size of an athletics track) of bamboo can store up to 250 tons of carbon (Qi, 2009). Using data on CO2 emissions from the World Bank, this translates into the amount of carbon that was produced in 2009 by around 160 people in China (or, equivalently, 50 people in the U.S.A.).  

Is unique. Is a work of art. It feels alive.

Using other materials like carbon fibers, aluminium or steel is convenient, easy, fast and they require less hand work. For this reason a bamboo bike an uncommon functional work of art to be seen on the streets.
While it looks all natural and it incorporates pure nature into it´s functional design, is also incredible strong and it dampens vibrations better than any other material. It also has a reduced weigh. All these factors and our carefully calculated frame geometry gives the rider a pleasant feeling of smoothness during the ride.
